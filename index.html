Cyber Essentials at Home

Hi everyone! My name is Mark Turner and I’m a Computer Security masters student at the University of Manchester.

My dissertation project, Cyber Essentials at Home, will help you improve your cyber security, especially now more of you are working from home. Want to get involved and help keep yourself safe? You will be asked to:


  1. Fill out a short survey
  2. Install a small piece of software on your computer which checks your cyber security (can be uninstalled at any time you wish)
  3. Watch a few videos and point out cyber security flaws

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to email me at: mark.turner-7@postgrad.manchester.ac.uk
